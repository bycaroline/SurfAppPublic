Surf Forecast Notification

Knäbäckshusen is located on the east coast of southern Sweden and is well-known by surfers in the area as a spot that at times can deliver great surfing conditions, for being in the Baltic Sea.

Since the Baltic Sea is rather narrow, the only times surf conditions can be favorable in Knäbäckshusen is when the wind is onshore for some time and then, either shifts to offshore, or decreases drastically. As expected this happens very rarely and in order to be able to spot such an instance one must constantly be monitoring surfing apps. By subscribing for this app you will be notified one day in advance for when surf might be favorable at Knäbäckshusen.
